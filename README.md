## About Me

I'm a CS undergrad at **Habib University** (full merit scholar) and co-founder of **[Unity Algo](https://github.com/Zafar205)** — an ERPNext/Frappe-based platform built for microfinance institutions and SMEs in Pakistan, with live paying clients and recurring revenue.

I freelance as a full-stack developer, have interned at **SUPARCO** (Pakistan's national space agency) automating antenna simulation pipelines with PyANSYS, and compete in **ICPC**. My long-term goal is to scale Unity Algo into a broader fintech infrastructure platform for emerging markets.
